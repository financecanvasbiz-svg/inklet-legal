# Community & Content Guidelines
**Last Updated:** June 17, 2026

Welcome to Inklet! We are built to be a safe, creative, and inspiring space for writers to publish stories and readers to discover them. To keep our community safe, every user is required to follow these guidelines.

By creating an account, posting stories, uploading story covers, or writing comments, you agree to these rules. We enforce a strict zero-tolerance policy for content or behavior that violates these guidelines.

---

## 1. Prohibited Content
You may not publish, upload, or share any of the following content anywhere on Inklet (including inside stories, story titles, story covers, or comments):

* **Plagiarism & Copyright Infringement:** You must own or have the explicit rights to any text or image you post. Do not post other people's work without permission.
* **Hate Speech & Harassment:** Content that attacks, intimidates, degrades, or incites violence against individuals or groups based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or age.
* **Explicit or Inappropriate Material:** Pornographic, highly graphic sexual content, or excessive gratuitous violence. Story covers must be safe for general audiences.
* **Bullying & Personal Attacks:** Targeted insults, stalking, trolling, or posting someone else's Private Identifiable Information (doxxing).
* **Spam & Deception:** Promoting commercial links, scamming users, or creating fake accounts to manipulate likes, comments, or views.

---

## 2. Age Requirement & Accountability
To protect younger users and comply with global digital privacy standards:
* You must be at least **13 years old** to create an account and participate on Inklet.
* You are solely responsible for the content you post and the interactions you have under your account.

---

## 3. Our Safety & Moderation Tools
We give our community the direct power to shape their experience. If you encounter abusive behavior or violating content, you have two primary built-in tools at your disposal:

### Reporting Content
Every story and comment features a **Report** flag/button. When you submit a report, our moderation team is instantly notified.
* **Our 24-Hour Commitment:** We review all reported content and user accounts within 24 hours of submission.
* If content is found to violate these guidelines, it will be removed permanently, and the offending account will face penalties.

### Blocking Users
If another user is harassing you or you simply do not wish to see their work, you can use the **Block** tool found on their profile or via the options menu.
* Blocking someone will instantly hide their stories, profile, and comments from your feed.
* The blocked user will not be notified that you blocked them.

---

## 4. Enforcement & Penalties
We monitor our platform and act quickly on community reports. Violating these guidelines will result in enforcement actions at our sole discretion, including:
* A formal warning via email.
* Mandatory removal of the violating content or story cover.
* Temporary suspension of account features (e.g., losing the ability to comment or post).
* Permanent account bans and IP blocking for severe or repeat offenders.

**Zero Tolerance:** Severe violations—such as uploading explicit material involving minors, targeted harassment campaigns, or extreme hate speech—will result in an **immediate, permanent account termination** on the very first offense, without warning or right to appeal.
