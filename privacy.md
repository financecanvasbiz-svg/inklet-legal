# Privacy Policy
**Last Updated:** June 17, 2026

At Inklet, your privacy is of paramount importance to us. This Privacy Policy outlines exactly how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and web platform.

By creating an account or using Inklet, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect
We only collect information that is necessary to provide you with a smooth, secure, and interactive storytelling experience.

### A. Personal Information You Provide Voluntarily
To register an individual account, post stories, upload story covers, leave comments, or like content, you must provide us with the following data:
* **Email Address:** Used for account authentication, password recovery, secure login verification via Firebase Auth, and critical platform notifications.
* **Date of Birth (Birthday):** Collected strictly for age verification. We do not display your exact birthdate or age publicly on your profile.
* **Self-Reported Location:** We collect the location details (such as country or region) that you voluntarily input or select during your profile setup. This is used strictly to customize your story feed and display relevant regional content.

### B. User-Generated Content (UGC)
When you use the social and publishing features of our app, we collect and store:
* The text of your published stories and comments.
* The images you upload as story covers.
* Your platform interactions (e.g., likes, saves, subscriptions, and who you choose to block).

### C. Cookies and Local Storage Tracking
* When you access our platform via a web browser, we use cookies, local storage tokens, and similar web technologies.
* These are strictly functional and necessary to securely maintain your logged-in session, remember your preferences, and protect our site against cross-site scripting attacks.
* We do not use tracking cookies for targeted third-party advertising.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use your data to operate the core functionalities of the application and comply with global safety regulations:
* **Authentication & Access:** To create your profile and manage your secure login status.
* **Content Customization:** Utilizing your location and platform likes to curate your personal "Up Next" feed and recommended lists.
* **Age-Gating Compliance:** Processing your birthday to guarantee that no users under the age of 13 can access interactive community features, ensuring compliance with COPPA and global children’s privacy acts.
* **Platform Safety & Moderation:** Processing user reports and block commands to filter malicious content and remove users who violate our Community Guidelines.

---

## 3. Third-Party Service Providers & Cloud Data Storage
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is stored securely using industry-standard infrastructure providers:
* **Google Firebase:** We use Firebase Authentication to secure your email and login tokens, Firebase Firestore to index your text stories, comments, likes, and block lists, and Firebase Cloud Storage to host your uploaded story covers. All cloud processing aligns with standard encrypted data protocols.

---

## 4. Content Visibility & Your Choices
Because Inklet is a social publishing community, please note how your data is distributed:
* **Public Profiles:** Your chosen username, published stories, uploaded story covers, and public comments are visible to all users of the platform.
* **Private Data:** Your account email address, exact date of birth, and precise location coordinate histories are never displayed to other users.

---

## 5. Data Retention & Account Deletion
We retain your personal data and User-Generated Content only for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with platform services.

### Your Right to Deletion
You have the complete right to delete your account and remove your personal footprint from the system. If you request account deletion through the app settings or via email, we will purge your personal identifiable data and delete your published stories and comments from our active Firebase databases within 30 days.

---

## 6. Security
We implement robust electronic safeguards via Firebase secure servers to guard against data loss or unauthorized access. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet or cloud storage solution is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
